# V0.1 Mechanical Prototype Sources

`TactileRide_V0.1_Reference_Model.scad` is a parametric static ergonomic
mock-up reference. Its envelope, controls, clamp, and USB opening are
provisional and must not be interpreted as final CAD or a printable production
release.

Before any physical mock-up, measure the intended motorcycle and use the
[V0.1 blueprint index](../../../docs/blueprints/v0.1/README.md) to record the
remaining clearance and reach work. Do not mount, evaluate, or use a prototype
while riding.
