# Project Goals

## Confirmed goals

- Enable eyes-free media control through tactile differentiation and muscle
  memory.
- Support operation while wearing motorcycle gloves without releasing the left
  grip.
- Minimise distraction through a small, simple action set.
- Use standard Bluetooth HID Consumer Control and automatic reconnection where
  the selected platform supports it.
- Favour low power, replaceable parts, and reproducible hardware.

## Current assumptions

- A dedicated controller can be simpler and less distracting than interacting
  with an on-screen media control.
- Separating firmware, electronics, and mechanical work will let the project
  evolve without prematurely coupling decisions.

## Open questions

- How will glove types and mounting positions affect reach and error rate?
- What reconnect latency and battery lifetime are acceptable for the prototype?
- Which environmental and vibration scenarios should define later targets?

## Deferred ideas

- Motorcycle electrical integration and vehicle data access.
- Expanded controls, navigation actions, or optional applications.

## Acceptance criteria

- Product decisions are traceable to requirements and ADRs.
- Future prototypes can be assessed against a documented ergonomic and bench
  test plan rather than anecdotal use alone.
